No Duplication

Ground Rules

🗂️ What lives in Drive

  • ILIA-Master-Context.md (primary AI context)
  • Living SOPs updated frequently
  • Working drafts before they're formal
  • Design assets, PDFs, videos
  • Meeting notes and decision logs

📘 What lives in Confluence

  • Formal feature specifications
  • Epics with JIRA ticket links
  • Architecture decision records
  • Release notes and changelogs
  • Anything needing comment threads

✅ Build Order

  • Step 1 — Approve folder structure (now)
  • Step 2 — Create the Shared Drive empty
  • Step 3 — Migrate existing files
  • Step 4 — Update Claude Project Instructions
  • Step 5 — Connect GitHub when Mohit delivers
  • Step 6 — Share with NIW team

🚫 Non-Negotiables

  • No file lives in both Drive and Confluence
  • No adding files without a designated home
  • Staging is temporary — not a storage folder
  • Confluence links to Drive; Drive doesn't link back
  • Structure approved before anything is created
